Who are we?


A Team, A family. We are Team E-Cell



Missions

Our mission is to instil the spirit of entrepreneurship among the young minds and provide a medium for entrepreneurial intellect and fetch conceivable changes. E-Cell KIET has taken extraordinary measures to strengthen the entrepreneurial culture and has succeeded in marking a place for itself.

We endure hard to achieve inspiring surroundings that promote entrepreneurship among the public by providing opportunities for mentorship, networking, and interacting with the eminent working professionals of the corporate world. At E-Cell, we share the expertise of our alumni and instructors who provide aids such as mentoring, networking with different entrepreneurs, and seed funding.


Visions


E-Cell KIET is a student body that helps to promote an entrepreneurial culture among the young minds of today's generation and to nurture the entrepreneurial mindset of aspiring individuals to recast their "Drop of an idea into an ocean of reality". We aim to bring forth the entrepreneurial desires to converge on a common platform.

Our vision is to foster students to convert their achievable ideas into self-sustaining enterprises, and to bring forth the entrepreneur residing within them. We aspire to augment proficiency in the field of management and innovative invention among the youth and to open a new window for their future.
